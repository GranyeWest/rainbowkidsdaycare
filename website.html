About Us

Welcome to Fauzia's Rainbow Kids Home Daycare! We offer a loving, safe, and educational environment for children to grow, learn, and play. Our experienced caregivers are dedicated to providing a fun, educational environment that helps foster development, creativity, and important values such as good manners, caring, sharing, patience, responsibility, communication, and teamwork. We also offer preschool activities such as learning the alphabet, shapes, colors, numbers, and name recognition.

Our Programs

  • Infant Care: Specialized care for infants, ages 0-18 months.
  • Toddler Program: Interactive play and early learning for toddlers, ages 18 months - 3 years.
  • Preschool: A structured, fun learning environment for children ages 3-5.

Field Trips

We love to go on field trips! Some of our favorite destinations include the local library, nearby parks, and Belchers Marsh pond and trails. Parent volunteers are always welcome and appreciated. For trips that require travel, we may use public transportation, and parents may be asked to provide car seats or pay associated fees. Safety is our priority, and all children will be in safety-approved car seats as needed.
